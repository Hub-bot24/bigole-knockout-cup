# BIGOLE Knockout Cup

Standalone, one-screen landscape finals bracket.

## Finals routing

- 1 v 4 winner goes directly to Preliminary Final 1; loser goes to Semi Final 1.
- 5 v 8 winner goes to Semi Final 1; loser is eliminated.
- 6 v 7 winner goes to Semi Final 2; loser is eliminated.
- 2 v 3 winner goes directly to Preliminary Final 2; loser goes to Semi Final 2.
- Semi Final 1 winner crosses to Preliminary Final 2.
- Semi Final 2 winner crosses to Preliminary Final 1.

The first-round ladder and later match scores load from `data/live.json`.
